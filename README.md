## Executive Summary

**Smart Fleet IoT Platform** is a business analysis case study based on the end-to-end design and delivery of a fleet management solution developed from scratch. The platform enables organizations to monitor connected vehicles in real time through IoT devices, validate telemetry data, optimize fleet operations, and support proactive maintenance.

This repository demonstrates how business requirements were transformed into functional specifications, user stories, business processes, and solution documentation while following Agile practices.

The primary objective of this case study is to showcase a complete Requirement Engineering process from business problem identification to solution definition and delivery.

---

## Business Problem

Fleet operators required a centralized platform capable of managing connected vehicles and transforming raw vehicle data into actionable business insights.

The existing operational model lacked a scalable solution for:

* Real-time vehicle monitoring
* Telemetry data validation
* Fleet utilization optimization
* Predictive maintenance support
* Eco-driving analysis
* Driver behavior monitoring
* Operational reporting

In addition, a newly developed IoT vehicle tracking device required an integrated platform capable of supporting installation, activation, monitoring, and ongoing fleet operations.

---

## Solution Overview

The Smart Fleet IoT Platform was designed as a cloud-based fleet management solution that integrates IoT devices installed in vehicles with a centralized monitoring platform.

The platform enables organizations to:

* Monitor vehicles in real time
* Validate incoming telemetry data
* Track vehicle installations
* Analyze driver behavior
* Monitor vehicle health
* Generate operational alerts
* Support preventive maintenance planning
* Produce fleet performance reports
